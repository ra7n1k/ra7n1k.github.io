  1. You must not destroy structures constructed by users other than yourself without permission.
  2. The Nether (world_nether) and the world_the_end are intended only for gathering resources. There is no obligation to protect structures unless specifically directed by the managers.
  3. Moderators have the authority to enter any area, and if there is a structure that violates the norms, they will be notified8 You can perform removal etc.
  4. When building a structure, it must be built in a place where the ground is at least 1 block away from the road and at least 3 blocks away from other buildings.
  5. The approximate land area available for one building is determined by the distance from the spawn point.
    (If you exceed the guidelines, please submit your plan to the support/general-building-support channel on the Discord server. please.)
    6. Less than 128 blocks from spawn point
    At least 1 block between lands, avoid contact, maximum land area is 21 x 29 blocks.
    Less than 512 blocks from spawn point
    At least 3 blocks between lands, avoid contact, maximum land area is 29 x 37 blocks.
    Less than 1024 blocks from spawn point
    At least 3 blocks between lands, avoid contact, maximum land area is 37 x 45 blocks.
    1025 blocks or more from spawn point
    Avoid contact, no land area restrictions.

    If the moderator determines that it is interfering with the use of others, it may be removed after notification.

  6. When constructing or renovating a structure on land, you must ensure that there are no regular blocks of ground on the site with a brightness of less than 1.

    7. If a monster spawns and you suffer a disadvantage, the moderator will investigate and you can request compensation from the landowner.

    Target disadvantage, compensation amount multiplier

    • Injuries caused by player being attacked, 10
    • Lethal due to player being attacked, 15
    • Injury due to attack of one mob owned by the player (does not take into account units that change depending on type), 5
    • Lethal due to attack of one mob owned by the player (does not take into account units that change depending on type), 8
    • Noise, 2

    Formula

    YS$(Ground area of normal blocks whose brightness is less than 1 among the target land × Multiplier of compensation amount for target disadvantage)

    Condition

    • The ground area of regular blocks with brightness less than 1 can be determined during the darkest time.
    • Continuous attacks are treated as one attack.
    • If you are injured by an attack and subsequently die, only lethality can be applied.
    • If there are multiple levels, the ground area can be counted for each level.
    • If there are multiple target disadvantages, multiply by adding each multiplier itself.

    Example

    Out of the 81 blocks of land 3, the ground of 98 blocks 3 is a normal block with a brightness of less than 1, and monsters spawned from it was attacked twice and a pet wolf was killed.

    YS$(98 × (10 + 8) = 1764)

    So in this case, you can claim YS$1764.
